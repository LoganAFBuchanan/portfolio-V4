Marvel Realm of Champions

Role: RPG Designer
Company: Kabam
Duration: Aug. 2019 - Jul.2021
Tools: Unity 3D, Excel/Sheets, Perforce, Jira

Overview

At Kabam I was an RPG Designer working on the now sunsetted mobile game Marvel Realm of Champions.

I designed over 20 unique gear sets totalling 100+ unique passives for our gameplay customization system 'Synergies'. These synergies add new emergent gameplay loops to each character's loadout and can be freely mixed and matched between eachother to create a vast amount of incredibly diverse combinations.

To learn more you can check out the game's website at MarvelRealmOfChampions.com
