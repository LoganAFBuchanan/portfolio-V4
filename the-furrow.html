The Furrow

Role: Systems Designer, Programmer
Team Members: Juliana Loughin, Quinn MacDonald
Duration: January 2018 - Present
Tools: Unity 3D (C#)

Overview

The Furrow is a turn based tactical rogue-like game in the style of FTL and Slay the Spire with an emphasis on telling a personal story about the travelers who brave its challenges.

This is my graduation project for my BSc in Interactive Arts & Technology from SFU. I am the projects Systems & Mechanics Designer as well as the sole programmer. My primary work at this point has been mechanic design, below you can find links to several of the design documents for the various systems in the game.
